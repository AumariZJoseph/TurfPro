About Turf Pro Landscaping

Turf Pro Landscaping is a leader in commercial lawn maintenance, surface and sub-surface drainage services across St. Lucia. Our main priority is the satisfaction of our customers. We are passionate about customer service.

We continuously work to improve our service through the use of modern equipment and continued hands-on training for our staff. We believe that our professionalism, quality service and reliability set us apart from our competitors.

Turf Pro Landscaping is run by professionals dealing with all aspects of landscaping and maintenance. For us, each job is a priority, whether big or small. We provide excellent landscape maintenance services in an efficient and timely manner.

Our landscape contractors leave the site only at the completion of the job, and the satisfaction of the customer. We adopt a very high level of commitment by concentrating on one job at a time.

Our excellent track record is due primarily to our knowledgeable and well-trained staff. Reliable and efficient, our team gives us an edge over others in the industry. We work strictly on the guidance and requirements of the customers.
